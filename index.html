Introduction


What is VoxelScape dataset?
The VoxelScape dataset is a large-scale simulated point cloud dataset of urban traffic environements. It contains total 100K point cloud LiDAR scans of diverse urban traffic scenarios and their ground-truth annotations. Each point cloud scan includes a fine-detailed point-wise semantic labels and reflectance intensity as well as 3D bounding boxes annotations.


Characteristics

  • 100 sequences (each with 1000 point cloud scans)
  • 13340 million annotated points
  • 32 Point-wise Semantic Class Labels
  • 9 Object Classes with 3D Bounding Boxes

Intensity Values

We simulate the reflection intensity of different surfaces by incorporating the incidence vector from the simulated sensor and the surface of the laser beam with a linear light fall-off attenuation and material properties. We obtained the reflectance parameters of different surface materials and used a standard 2D Gaussian distribution to simulate the fine grains of the material.

Point-Wise Semantic Labels

We provide the largest point cloud dataset with point-wise semantic labels of 32 class labels. The class labels include the following: buildings, road, sidewlak, car, vegetation, adult pedestrians, child pedestrians, crosswalk, truck, pole, curb, terrain, bus stop, bicyclist, lane-marking, parking, trash-can, bicycle, mototrcyclist, motorcycle, seat, mailbox, fire-hydrant, traffic-sign, construction-barrier, phone-booth, construction-cone, fence and traffic-light.

3D Bounding Boxes

We proivde also 3D bounding boxes of 9 different object classes: adult pedestrians, child pedestrians, cars, trucks, buses, cyclists, motorcyclists, contruction barriers and construction cones.
